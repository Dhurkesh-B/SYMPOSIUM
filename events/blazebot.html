Stepping Off the Screen and Into the Real World


    An engaging TEDx-style contest focused on the transformative impact of Artificial~ Intelligence and Robotics in real-world applications. This will challenge participants to explore the depths of AI innovation and present thought-provoking insights in a two-round competition.

Round 1: Prelims

    TEDx Talk Presentation Participants will deliver a compelling TEDx-style talk on the topics:
  1.   AI: Stepping Off the Screen and Into the Real World.
  2.   AI being incredibly smarter and shockingly stupid
  3.   AI generated creatures stretching the boundary of imagination
  4.   Will super intelligence end the world?
  5.   AI and Robotics Reconnecting Us to Humanity
  6.   AI on second industrial revolution
  7.   Generative AI - A Way of Life.

Round 2: On-Spot Challenge

    Finalists will be given an impromptu topic related to AI or Robotics and will have one minute to prepare before delivering a three-minute speech. This round will test their ability to think on their feet, articulate ideas clearly, and engage the audience effectively.

Judging Criteria:

    Winners will be selected based on the following key factors:
  1.   Originality - Unique and innovative perspectives on AI and Robotics.
  2.   Relevance to Current Issues - Addressing modern challenges and future implications.
  3.   Passion and Delivery - Engaging with confidence and clarity.
  4.   Structure and Insightfulness - Well-organized ideas supported by logical evidence.
  5.   Audience Engagement - Effective storytelling, interaction, and captivating speech techniques.
